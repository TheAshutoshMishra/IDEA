## ğŸ¤ Delivery Tips

### Voice & Body Language:
- Speak clearly and confidently
- Make eye contact with judges
- Use hand gestures for emphasis
- Show enthusiasm!

### Technical Presentation:
- Explain concepts simply (assume non-technical judges)

- Listen carefully, don't interrupt

- Have "skip slides" ready if running over
- Most important: Slides 5 & 6 (insights + demo)
